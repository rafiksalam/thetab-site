The Tab

* Support *

The Tab is a bill-splitting app for iPhone: log shared expenses, scan receipts, split fairly among friends and groups, and settle up.

Contact

Common questions

How do I delete my account?
In the app: Friends tab → Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and data, including entries you created on friends' tallies.

How are balances calculated?
Every expense records who paid and each person's share. Your balance with a friend is the running net of those shares across all entries, including group expenses.

What happens to receipt photos I scan?
They're processed once to read the items and totals, then discarded — the app keeps only the itemized text. See the privacy policy.

A balance looks wrong.
Open the friend or group, review the history, and tap any entry to see its split. Anyone in an entry can view it; the person who logged it can edit or delete it.
